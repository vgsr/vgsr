VGSR is WP software, made for vgsr.nl

== Description ==

VGSR is designed, used and maintained to support vgsr.nl and bring in to its full potential for the glory of the VGSR community as a whole and her members specifically.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'VGSR'
3. Activate VGSR from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download VGSR.
2. Upload the 'vgsr' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate VGSR from your Plugins page.
